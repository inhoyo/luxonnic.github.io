TECHNOLOGY

Optical machine learning,
on a single chip.

A programmable photonic neural network that approximates any nonlinear transformation, computed entirely in the optical domain.

ORIGIN

Born at Fraunhofer HHI.

LUXONNIC is a spin-off in the making from the Photonic Networks and Systems department at Fraunhofer Heinrich Hertz Institute, one of Europe's leading communications research institutes. The founding team is still rooted there, building the PNP on the institute's deep expertise in photonics and signal processing.

Dr.-Ing. candidate, TU Berlin (submission June 2026). Researcher at Fraunhofer HHI in the Photonic Networks & Systems department, formerly specializing in THz wireless communications, probabilistic constellation shaping, and real-time DSP. Holds a world record in 300 GHz dual-polarization THz transmission — 221.5 Gb/s over 500 m — the longest-range, highest-capacity photonic THz link demonstrated at this frequency band. Alumnus of KIT (Karlsruhe) and KAIST, Baden-Württemberg Scholar, lecturer at Berlin Universities since 2022, and winner of the Best Student Paper Award at OECC 2026 (Busan). Invited speaker at Seoul National University (June 2026). Active in Korean–German R&D partnership strategy spanning TIPA, Horizon Europe, and bilateral instruments.
