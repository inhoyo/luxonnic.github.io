TECHNOLOGY

Optical machine learning,
on a single chip.

A programmable photonic neural network that approximates any nonlinear transformation, computed entirely in the optical domain.

ORIGIN

Born at Fraunhofer HHI.

LUXONNIC is a spin-off in the making from the Photonic Networks and Systems department at Fraunhofer Heinrich Hertz Institute, one of Europe's leading communications research institutes. The founding team is still rooted there, building the PNP on the institute's deep expertise in photonics and signal processing.
