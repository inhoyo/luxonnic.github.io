WHY NOW

AI factories are hitting a power wall.

Every GPU cluster in an AI factory is connected by optical transceivers. Those transceivers already consume up to 40 MW per facility — and the DSPs inside them scale their hunger with every bandwidth upgrade. The industry cannot keep building this way.

HOW IT WORKS

A neural network built from light itself.

Our photonic integrated circuit performs signal equalization in the optical domain — no analog-to-digital conversion, no digital computation. Trained in-situ, reconfigurable, and WDM-native. DSP-grade performance at under 1 W.

Designed for where DSP is the bottleneck.
